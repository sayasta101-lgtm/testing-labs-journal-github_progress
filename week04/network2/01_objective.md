
## 📘 1. Objective (In Your Own Words)
Briefly describe the purpose of this lab.  
What were you trying to achieve?
The purpose of this lab was to analyse how an AI-based recruitment system can create unfair outcomes when it is trained on biased historical data. The aim was to evaluate the legal, ethical, and social implications of using AI in hiring decisions and to determine whether the system should continue to be used. Through this case study, I sought to identify the risks of indirect discrimination, assess the impact on different stakeholders, and recommend ways to make the recruitment process fairer and more responsible.
