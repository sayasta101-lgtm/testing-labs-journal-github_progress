## 📘 1. Summary of the Task
Describe the purpose of this activity in your own words:  
- What is the goal of the lab?  
- What network are you expected to create?

_Write your summary here…_

The purpose of this activity is to learn how computer networks are designed, configured, and tested. The goal of the lab is to understand how different networking devices and computers communicate with each other and how data is transferred across a network. During the lab, you are expected to create a small local area network (LAN) by connecting devices such as PCs, switches, and routers. You will configure network settings, assign IP addresses, and verify connectivity between devices. This helps develop practical networking skills and provides a better understanding of how real-world networks operate and support communication between computers.
